Common Questions

What is your minimum order quantity? +

Standard corrugated boxes: MOQ 500 units. Custom-printed packaging: MOQ 1,000 units. Sample orders available for quality verification at nominal charges.

How quickly can I get a quote? +

For standard products with clear specifications, quotes are ready within 4 business hours. For complex custom requirements, quotes are provided within 24–48 hours.

Do you provide samples before bulk orders? +

Yes. We provide pre-production samples (PPS) for approval before bulk production. Sample charges range from ₹500–2,000 depending on complexity, adjustable against first order.

What are your payment terms? +

New clients: 100% advance. Established clients (after 3 orders): 50% advance, 50% against delivery. Credit terms available for large-volume clients on credit assessment.

Can I visit your factory before placing an order? +

Absolutely! We welcome factory visits Mon–Sat, 10 AM–4 PM. Please schedule in advance by calling +91 79 2589 0000 or emailing sales@packnovaindustries.com.
