Our Pledge

Built on Environmental Responsibility

At PackNova, sustainability is embedded in every business decision — from raw materials sourced to energy consumed and waste generated. We continuously measure, improve, and report our environmental impact.

Our Green 2030 roadmap targets carbon neutrality in manufacturing, 100% recyclable product portfolio, and zero-landfill waste by end of this decade.

Our Programs

Green Initiatives

☀️
Solar Energy Programme

500 kW rooftop solar installation generates 7 lakh units of clean electricity annually, displacing 580 tonnes of CO₂ equivalent emissions. Targeting 1 MW by 2026.

Progress to 1 MW goal50%
♻️
Circular Waste Economy

85% of manufacturing waste recycled or upcycled. Paper trims go to our in-house pulping unit. Ink sludge sent to certified co-processors. Target: zero-landfill by 2027.

Waste diversion rate85%
🌲
FSC Responsible Sourcing

100% of paper and board inputs from FSC Chain-of-Custody certified mills, ensuring responsible forest management and no deforestation across our supply chain.

FSC-certified sourcing100%
💧
Zero Liquid Discharge

Closed-loop water recycling treats and recycles 100% of process water, achieving zero liquid discharge. Fresh water consumption reduced by 60% since 2018.

🌿
Eco-Friendly Inks

Water-based and UV-curable inks instead of solvent-based across all printing lines, eliminating VOC emissions and making packaging fully recyclable.

🚚
Green Logistics

Route optimization reduces delivery kilometres by 18%. Transitioning to CNG/EV vehicles for last-mile delivery, targeting 50% green fleet by 2025.
