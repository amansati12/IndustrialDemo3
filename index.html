About PackNova

Engineered for Industrial Strength

From corrugated masterboxes protecting pharmaceuticals to premium printed e-commerce boxes, our solutions combine engineering precision with aesthetic appeal.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +

Our MOQ varies by product. For standard corrugated boxes: 500 units. For custom printed packaging: 1,000 units. Sample orders available for quality verification.

Do you provide custom packaging design services? +

Yes! Our in-house design team creates custom packaging solutions including dieline design, structural engineering, and graphic design. We accept AI, PDF, or CDR artwork files.

What certifications does PackNova hold? +

PackNova holds ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015, FSC Chain-of-Custody certification, and Food Safety Management certification for our food-grade packaging lines.

What is your typical production lead time? +

Standard products ship within 7-10 business days. Custom-designed packaging typically requires 15-21 business days from artwork approval.

Do you offer pan-India delivery? +

Yes, we deliver across all 28 states and 8 Union Territories of India. We also handle export packaging for international shipments.
