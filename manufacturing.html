Process

From Raw Material to Ready Product

01
Raw Material Sourcing

FSC-certified kraft liner and fluting paper from certified mills. Every incoming lot tested for GSM, moisture, and burst strength before entering production.

02
Design & Pre-Press

CAD structural dieline design optimized for material efficiency. CTP plate making ensures accurate colour reproduction and registration.

03
Corrugation & Board Making

Automatic corrugation line at 150 m/min. Single-facer, double-facer, and slitter-scorer produce boards of precise flute profile.

04
Printing & Finishing

8-colour flexographic press with Pantone matching. UV coating, matte/gloss lamination, hot foil stamping, and embossing available.

05
Die-Cutting & Creasing

Flatbed and rotary die-cutting with ±0.3mm accuracy. CNC die-makers produce custom dies for complex box styles and special cuts.

06
Quality Inspection

100% visual inspection and random sampling for BCT, ECT, FCT, and moisture content. NABL-accredited lab provides test certificates for every batch.

07
Packing & Dispatch

ERP-integrated dispatch system generates traceability documents. Bundled, strapped, palletized products shipped via our pan-India logistics network.
