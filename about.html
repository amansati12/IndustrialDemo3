Who We Are

Building the Backbone of India's Supply Chain

PackNova Industries Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2005 by Mr. Harshad Patel with a singular vision: to provide Indian businesses with world-class packaging solutions at competitive prices. What started as a 2,000 sq.ft. corrugated box unit has grown into a 20,000 sq.ft. integrated packaging facility employing 350+ skilled professionals.

Today, we serve 500+ clients across 7 major industries — from pharmaceutical giants to boutique e-commerce brands — delivering packaging that is engineered to protect, designed to impress, and built to last.

Our Journey

18 Years of Milestones

From humble beginnings to industry leadership — a story of grit, innovation, and relentless pursuit of quality.

₹120 Cr
Turnover in 2023 — from ₹12L in 2005
1,000x growth in 18 years
2005
Company Founded

Mr. Harshad Patel established PackNova with a 2,000 sq.ft. unit and 12 employees in GIDC Vatva, Ahmedabad.

2008
First Major Export Order

Secured first export packaging contract worth ₹2 Cr for a pharmaceutical client shipping to Europe.

2011
ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification, establishing formal quality management processes.

2015
Plant Expansion — Phase II

Expanded to 12,000 sq.ft. Added corrugated board plant and 5-colour flexo press printing unit.

2018
Eco-Friendly Line Launched

Launched India's most affordable FSC-certified eco-friendly packaging range, targeting sustainable businesses.

2021
Digital Transformation

Implemented Industry 4.0 automation, ERP integration, and online ordering portal. Capacity doubled.

2023
20,000 sq.ft. Mega Facility

Expanded to 20,000 sq.ft. with latest European machinery. Workforce grew to 350+. Turnover crossed ₹120 Cr.

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities

Automated Production Lines

6 fully automated corrugation and die-cutting lines with Industry 4.0 integration for consistent precision output.

Advanced Printing Unit

8-colour flexographic press with UV coating, hot foil stamping, and embossing for premium packaging.

Quality Testing Lab

NABL-accredited testing laboratory for BCT, ECT, moisture resistance, and burst strength testing.

Warehousing Facility

5,000 sq.ft. climate-controlled warehouse with FIFO inventory system for raw material and finished goods.

Solar Power Plant

500 kW rooftop solar installation powering 40% of our manufacturing operations, reducing carbon footprint.

Waste Recycling Plant

In-house paper waste recycling unit converting manufacturing trims into recycled raw material.
